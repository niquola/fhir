  <description value="Basic Profile. A documentation of healthcare-related information that is assembled together
   into a single statement of meaning that establishes its own context. A document is composed
   of a set of resources that include both human and computer readable portions. A human
   may attest to the accuracy of the human readable portion and may authenticate and/or sign
   the entire whole. A document may be kept as a set of logically linked resources, or they
   may be bundled together in an atom feed."/>
